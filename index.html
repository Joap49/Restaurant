Alfredo's Italian Restaurant

authentic italian food for half the price

Alfredo's Italain Restaurant was opened in 2003 by Alfredo Pilika. Alfredo's primarily works with take-out orders, but offers dine-in options too. Customers adore the authentic italian food which Alfredo learned how to cook during his 10 years in Abruzzo. He was the Head Chef at Zenobi, a quaint restaurant in San Benedetto.
